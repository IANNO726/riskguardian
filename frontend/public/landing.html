❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does it work with all MT5 brokers? +
Yes. Works with 50+ brokers including Deriv, IC Markets, XM, Exness, FP Markets, Pepperstone, and more. If your broker supports MT5, RiskGuardian works with it.
What are the 20 prop firm plans? +
FTMO (2-Step Phase 1 & 2, 1-Step, Swing), The5ers (Hyper Growth, High Stakes Phase 1 & 2, Bootcamp), FundedNext (Stellar 2-Step Phase 1 & 2, 1-Step, Lite, Instant), FundingPips (Standard Phase 1 & 2, Pro Phase 1, 1-Step), E8 Markets (Classic Phase 1 & 2, E8 One). All verified from official firm websites in March 2026.
What's the AI Behavioral Detection? +
It calculates a Discipline Score (0–100) from your trade history. It detects revenge trading (elevated lot size after consecutive losses), overtrading (5+ trades in a session), and emotional patterns. A detailed report is sent every Sunday via Telegram and email.
How does the news calendar work? +
It pulls from ForexFactory's free JSON feed — no API key needed. High-impact events (NFP, CPI, FOMC, rate decisions, PMI) are shown in the Simulator trade form and as alerts in the main dashboard. Events within 30 minutes show as red warnings. The calendar refreshes every hour.
What's included in the weekly report? +
Win rate, net P&L, average win/loss, expectancy per trade, discipline score, revenge trade count, risk lock events, best and worst day, top symbols by P&L, and a coaching insight. Delivered every Sunday at 8 PM via Telegram (Markdown) and email (styled HTML). Configurable via /api/v1/report/configure.
Can I compare multiple challenge simulations? +
Yes. Save up to 10 completed challenge sessions. Compare up to 5 side-by-side. You'll see overlaid equity curves, a metric-by-metric comparison table with "★ BEST" badges, and an overall winner banner. Available on Pro and above.
Is my broker login and data secure? +
Security is our top priority. Credentials are encrypted with AES-256. We use read-only API access only for monitoring — no one can place trades for you. All data is transmitted over TLS 1.3. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
Can I cancel or change my plan? +
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime — no fees. Access continues until the end of your billing period. Annual plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee.
