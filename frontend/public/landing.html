❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Does RiskGuardian work with my broker? ▾
If your broker supports MetaTrader 5, yes. We support 50+ MT5 brokers including Deriv, ICMarkets, Pepperstone, FTMO demo, and most major retail brokers. Deriv accounts connect via WebSocket — no MT5 required.
Can RiskGuardian place trades on my behalf? ▾
No. RiskGuardian is read-only. We monitor your account, send alerts, and can block trading access at the platform level — but we cannot open, modify, or close trades on your account. Your money is always under your control.
How does the prop firm simulator work? ▾
You select a firm and plan (e.g., FTMO 2-Step $100k), then simulate your trading day using real pip math and live ForexFactory news. The system tracks your pass probability, flags rule violations, and triggers psychological challenges — exactly like a live challenge.
Is the 7-day free trial really free? ▾
Yes. No credit card required. You get full access to your plan's features for 7 days. At the end, you choose to continue or not. We don't auto-charge you.
How does the kill switch actually stop trading? ▾
When your daily loss or drawdown limit is breached, RiskGuardian sends your MT5 platform a signal to reject new orders, plus instantly notifies you via Telegram. The stop is enforced at the API level — not just an alert you can ignore.
